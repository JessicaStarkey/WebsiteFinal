Books

The majority of my life I've loved reading. It started when I was around seven years of age. My mom was in college to become a registered nurse. As such, her textbooks were always laying around the house. Eventually, she tells me that I took a strong interest in them, and would always ask to read them. Odd material for a seven year old, no? Yet, still to this day, I enjoy reading psychology and medical textbooks. These were only the beginning, though.

I believe the book that officially sucked me into being an avid reader of fiction is called The Girl Who Loved Tom Gordon by Stephen King. It was a book that my mom owned. While bored one evening, around the age of ten, I decided to give it a shot. I did not regret it.

Down below, you will find a few of my favorite authors, as well as a quote from each. Also, each book listed under them are books that I have personally read and thoroughly enjoyed.

To really ask is to open the door to the whirlwind. The answer may annihilate the question and the questioner."
